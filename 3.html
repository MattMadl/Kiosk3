The “For You” page dominates TikTok. It’s where most scrolling is done. The “For You” page is a prime example that technology knows what we like more than we do.

The algorithm COULD be perfected, but isn’t. Every once in a while it tries something new. If you like the new thing, they’ll show you more. If not, it only makes seeing the things you like sweeter.

First impressions are critical. Talented creators and celebrities dominate TikTok thanks to their understanding and exploitation of the algorithm.
