Lets look at a few studies:

Studies by University of Kent School of Psychology indicate that users often struggle with keeping track of time when using TikTok.

Studies by Microsoft with assistance and publishing from Computers in Human Behavior found that users experienced a decrease in attention span within 20 minutes of using TikTok.

Depression rates are connected to TikTok usage, but the distinct cause is not yet understood. These findings could be more related to the content itself.
