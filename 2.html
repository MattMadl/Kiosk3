  • What Sets TikTok Apart?


Other than video length, TikTok is unique in that it requires videos to be vertical.

Vertical videos fit our phones better. Plus, we don’t have to rotate or hold our phone with two hands.

Try this: Hold your phone horizontally and try to press a like button on the opposite side with one hand. Difficult? See, that’s just horrible design. TikTok and other dominant short form content platforms avoid this problem.
