  • If You Can't Beat Them, Join Them


Instagram, Facebook, YouTube and Pinterest have replicated TikTok’s UI choices.

The heavyweights have adapted to not die out. Social media sites like MySpace, Vine, and Tumblr refused to update and optimize based on the competitors. Meta (Facebook and Instagram) does not want to meet the same fate.

The like, comment, share and follow features are all in the same place on every platform. This gives ease of use to right handers.

You’ll notice some small differences in icons. These subtle changes are made to maintain a perfect UI, but also to dodge legal trouble with competitors.
